Key Lime Pie

Smooth and tangy key lime pie recipe for your exquisite tastes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 6 tbsp melted butter
  • 3 cans sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup key lime juice
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tbsp lime zest

Steps

  1. Prebake crust @350Â°F for 8 min
  2. Whisk filling ingredients
  3. Pour into cooled crust
  4. Bake 15-18 mins until set
  5. Chill 3+ hours
  6. Add whipped cream toppings and lime zest
