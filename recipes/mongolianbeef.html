Mongolian Beef

This is one of the crunchiest and spiciest Mongolian Beef recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs of flank steak, sliced thin against the grain
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Pinch red pepper flakes
  • Sliced green onions

Steps

  1. Coat steak with cornstarch
  2. Sear steak in oil & remove
  3. Mix sauce ingredients
  4. Boil sauce in pan
  5. Add steak back and simmer
  6. Garnish with green onions
  7. Serve over rice
